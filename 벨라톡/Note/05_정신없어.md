## 1. be busy ing 

- I'm busy working these days.
- I'm busy right now.

## 2. Be tied up

- 일정에 묶여 있음, 시간 여유 없음 (업무 / 일정 느낌)

- I'm tied up with work. 

- He's tied up in meetings all day.

- I'm tied up at the moment.


## 3. Be swamped

- 완전 정신없음

- He's completely swamped.

- I've been so swamped.
