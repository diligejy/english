## 1. 영어는 "눈치 본다"라고 직접 말하지 않고 왜 조심했는지 이유로 설명하는 게 자연스러움.

## 2. 분위기 / 반응을 파악하면서 맞추는 중 

- I'm trying to read the room.

- I'm trying to read the room before I bring it up.

- I was just reading the room.

## 3. 괜히 선 넘는 사람 될까봐 / 누군가 기분 상하게 할까봐 조심 

- I don't want to step on anyone's toes.

- I didn't say anything. I didn't want to step on anyone's toes.

- I'm not trying to step on your toes.

## 4. 어색해질까봐 / 분위기 이상해질까봐 

- I don't want to make it awkward.

- I didn't say anything. I didn't want to make it awkward.

- I don't want to make things awkward between us.
