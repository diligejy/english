    - He takes guitar lessons twice a week.

3. 나 이 수업 작년에 들었어.

    - I took this class last year.

4. 3개월째 받고 있어.

    - It's been three months.

5. 정말 긴 하루였어.

    - It's been a long day.

    - I know it's not your fault, but I'm still mad at you.
