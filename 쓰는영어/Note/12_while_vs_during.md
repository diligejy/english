## 1.  While (접속사) + 문장

- ~하는 동안, ~ 하다가, ~ 하면서, ~ 하는 중에

- 나는 운전하는 동안 절대 핸드폰을 보지 않아.

    - I never look at my phone while I'm driving.

    - I folded the laundry while watching TV.

    - You called me while I was sleeping.


## 2. During (전치사) + (이벤트) 명사

    - I fell asleep during the movie.

    - You can't use your phone during the test.

    - Refrain from speaking during the play.
